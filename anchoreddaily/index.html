Today's Verse

"We have this hope as an anchor for the soul, firm and secure."
— Hebrews 6:19 (NIV)

Reflection

An anchor doesn't stop a storm. It doesn't make the water calm or the wind quiet. What it does is hold you in place when everything around you is moving.

That's what hope in Christ is. Not a promise that life gets easy — but something deep and immovable that keeps you from drifting when it doesn't. The anchor holds even when you can't see the bottom.

Today, whatever you're facing, you're not adrift. You're held.

A Prayer

Lord, thank you that my hope is not in circumstances but in you. When life is uncertain, remind me that I am anchored to something that cannot move. Give me peace today. Amen.
