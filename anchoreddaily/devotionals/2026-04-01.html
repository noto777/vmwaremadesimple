Strength

"I can do all this through him who gives me strength."
— Philippians 4:13 (NIV)

Reflection

Context matters here. Paul wrote this from prison. He wasn't talking about crushing a workout or landing a deal. He was talking about contentment in any situation — plenty or want, free or chained.

The strength Christ gives isn't always the strength to win. Sometimes it's the strength to endure with peace intact. To stay grateful when things are hard. To not lose yourself when circumstances try to strip you down.

Whatever today holds — you have access to a strength that isn't yours.

A Prayer

Lord, I don't need easy. I need your strength. Give me what I need to face today with peace and purpose. Amen.
