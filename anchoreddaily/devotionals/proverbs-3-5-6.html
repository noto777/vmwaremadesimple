Trust

Scripture

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight."
— Proverbs 3:5-6 (NIV)

Reflection

"Lean not on your own understanding." That's the hard part. We're wired to figure things out, to map every step before we take it. But sometimes the map isn't ours to draw.

Trusting God with all your heart means releasing the outcome — not the effort, not the responsibility, but the outcome. You do your part. He straightens the path.

The person who submits their plans to God doesn't stop planning. They just stop white-knuckling the results.

A Prayer

Lord, I give you today's decisions. Where my understanding falls short, fill in the gaps. Make my paths straight. I trust you. Amen.
