Faith

Scripture

"Now faith is the assurance of things hoped for, the conviction of things not seen."
— Hebrews 11:1 (ESV)

Reflection

Faith isn't the absence of doubt. It's moving forward anyway — with assurance that what you can't see is more real than what you can.

The writer of Hebrews doesn't say faith is optimism, or a feeling, or a personality trait. It's a conviction. Something settled and decided, not dependent on circumstances cooperating.

You don't need to see the whole road. You just need to know who's leading.

A Prayer

Lord, grow my faith. Not the feeling kind — the convicted kind. Help me walk in assurance of what I cannot yet see. Amen.
