Strength

Scripture

"Do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you, surely I will help you, surely I will uphold you with My righteous right hand."
— Isaiah 41:10 (NASB)

Reflection

Three times God says "I will" in this verse. Not "I might." Not "if you're good enough." I will strengthen. I will help. I will uphold.

The command not to fear isn't based on circumstances improving — it's based on who is with you. Fear loses its grip when you're convinced you're not alone.

Whatever you're walking into today, you've got backup you didn't earn and can't lose.

A Prayer

God, I choose not to fear today. Not because things are easy, but because you said you'd hold me up. I'm trusting that. Amen.
