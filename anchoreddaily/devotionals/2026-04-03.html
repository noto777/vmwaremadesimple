Peace

"Be still, and know that I am God."
— Psalm 46:10 (NIV)

Reflection

Six words. But they cut through everything. In a world that rewards constant motion, constant output, constant noise — God says stop. Be still.

Stillness is where you remember who's actually in charge. The rushing, the striving, the anxiety — they all assume it's on you to make things work. Stillness says: it's not.

You don't have to have it figured out today. You just have to know who does.

A Prayer

Lord, quiet the noise in me today. Let me be still long enough to remember that you are God and I am not. Amen.
