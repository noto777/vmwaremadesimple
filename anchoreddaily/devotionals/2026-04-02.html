Courage

"Have I not commanded you? Be strong and courageous. Do not be frightened, and do not be dismayed, for the Lord your God is with you wherever you go."
— Joshua 1:9 (ESV)

Reflection

God said this to Joshua the day he had to step into Moses' shoes and lead an entire nation into unknown territory. No small task. No room for hesitation.

The command wasn't 'feel brave.' It was 'be strong and courageous' — an act of will, not an emotion. Courage isn't the absence of fear. It's moving despite it, because of who's behind you.

The reason given is simple: God is with you. Wherever you go. Not some of the places. All of them.

A Prayer

God, make me courageous today. Not because I have it figured out, but because you're with me. That's enough. Amen.
