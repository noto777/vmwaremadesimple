Soul Training

The Plan

Same principle as the gym. You don't start with 300 lbs on the bar. You build the pattern first, then add weight. Most Bible habits fail because people start too heavy. This plan doesn't.

Phase 1 Weeks 1–4 · Build the Habit

5 minutes. One verse. Every morning.

The goal isn't volume. It's showing up.

  • →Read today's verse on Anchored Daily right after your first coffee
  • →Read the reflection. Pray the prayer.
  • →That's it. Don't skip. Don't add more yet.

Book this month

Proverbs — 31 chapters, one per day

Short, direct, immediately applicable. Perfect starting point.

Phase 2 Weeks 5–8 · Add Weight

10–15 minutes.

Habit is locked in. Now build on it.

  • →Proverbs chapter of the day (still)
  • →One Psalm — read slowly
  • →One chapter of John — start at the beginning
  • →Write one sentence about what hit you

Why John first

Written specifically so you'll believe. Clear, narrative, accessible. Best entry point into the Gospels.

Phase 3 Month 3+ · Real Lifting

20–30 minutes.

This is the rhythm. The Word is no longer a discipline — it's a need.

  • →Full chapter from wherever you are in the New Testament
  • →One Proverb
  • →One Psalm
  • →One question: "What does this ask of me today?"

Non-Negotiables

  • Same time, same place. Inconsistency kills habits before they form. Pick a slot and protect it.
  • Never miss twice. Miss a day — fine. Miss two in a row and you're building a different habit.
  • Phone out of the bedroom. Put a physical Bible where your phone used to be. Remove the competition.
  • Volume isn't the goal. 5 focused minutes beats 30 distracted ones every time.
