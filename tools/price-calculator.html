📌 Pricing Sources & Methodology

VMware VVF / VCF
Broadcom list pricing via partner channel. VVF: $150–190/core/yr (shown at $170 mid-range). VCF: $350/core/yr. 72-core minimum enforced per Broadcom's Jan 2024 SKU restructuring. Annual subscription only — perpetual licensing is dead.
Proxmox VE
Community edition: free (AGPL open source). Standard sub: €110/socket/yr (~$119 USD). Enterprise sub: €440/socket/yr (~$475 USD). No per-core or per-VM licensing. Ceph storage software is free; only hardware costs apply.
Nutanix AHV
AHV hypervisor: free with any Nutanix license. NCI/NUS (enterprise features): ~$100–200/node/yr for small environments. Prism Pro: ~$100/node/yr add-on. Pricing is per-node, not per-core.
Microsoft Hyper-V
WS2025 Standard: $1,069/2-core pack, min 16 cores/server. Datacenter: $7,407/16-core + $463/additional 2-core pack. Shown as 3-yr amortized. CALs ($38/user or $19/device) are NOT included but are required.
